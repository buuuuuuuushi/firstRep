ABOUT US

A&A Events is an event planning and coordination company specializing in weddings and other special occasions. We are committed to helping clients create memorable and stress-free celebrations through professional planning, seamless coordination, and personalized service. From consultations and venue visits to vendor coordination and event execution, A&A Events ensures that every detail is carefully planned and managed to deliver exceptional experiences.

OUR STORY

It all began with a dream in 2015, when Patrice envisioned building a business she could proudly call her own. Standing beside her every step of the way was Robert, her devoted boyfriend at the time, who believed in her dream just as deeply as she did. Together, they turned that vision into reality by founding A&A Events in 2017.

The journey was far from easy. As fresh college graduates, they were often met with doubt. After all, who would entrust one of the most important days of their lives to a young couple with little experience? Yet, through passion, dedication, and countless hours of hard work, they gradually earned the trust of every client they served. One wedding after another, they transformed dreams into unforgettable celebrations, proving that genuine commitment speaks louder than years of experience.

In 2020, Robert and Patrice celebrated a milestone of their own as they exchanged vows in a civil wedding. A year later, they welcomed their beautiful daughter, adding another chapter to the life they had been building together. While they continued creating magical weddings for countless couples, one dream remained close to their hearts: celebrating their own church wedding.

Now, in 2026, after nine years of love, perseverance, and unwavering partnership, Robert and Patrice are preparing to fulfill that long-awaited dream. The same hands that carefully crafted the love stories of so many couples are finally writing the next chapter of their own. Their journey is a testament to the power of faith, resilience, and love that never stopped believing. If Robert and Patrice could turn their own dreams into reality despite every challenge they faced, they will pour that same passion, care, and dedication into bringing your dream wedding to life. Because to them, every love story deserves to be celebrated with the same magic they have always believed in, including their own.
